Lasagna

Description

This recipe is lovingly called "World's Best Lasagna". John Chandler created this lasagna recipe and shared it with Allrecipes.com over twenty years ago. It is consistantly one of the site's most popular recipes, as well as one of the most popular lasagna recipes on the internet. The recipe is prepared in the traditional manner, and though it takes some time to prepare, the finished dish is worth the wait. Users also love this recipe because of how customizable it is.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Cook the meat sauce. Cook the sausage, beef, onion, and garlic until well browned. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Next add the sugar, half of the parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon of the salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and the egg. Then mix in the remaining parsley and salt.
  4. To assemble, the ingredients must be layered in a pan.
    • Put about 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce in the bottom of the pan.
    • Lay 6 of the noodles length-wise across the meat sauce.
    • Spread about half of the ricotta mixture on top of the noodles.
    • Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.
    • Spoon 1/2 cup meat sauce on top ot the cheese slices and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan.
    • Repeat layers and top with remaining cheeses.
    • Cover with foil sprayed with cooking spray.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for 25 more minutes. Let lasagna rest for at least 15 minutes before serving.
